~ Journey to the Unknown ~

Do you feel like life is full of uncertainties? You never know what will go right or wrong. What if you are not sure how your life will turn out? I am scared just like most college students who feel the overwhelming pressure of the real world. I guess I’m one of them now.

Entering college feels like a trial version of the real world. By “real world,” I mean working, collaborating with different people, taking roles seriously, and being responsible for tasks. As a Computer Science student, I believe there are many opportunities in this field. But what if I still don’t know where I truly belong? The pressure of being in my second year while not yet mastering my field sometimes bothers me. However, one thing is certain — I will finish college. That is a promise not just to myself, but to my parents. Just like an algorithm in the computer world, I need a plan so I won’t feel stuck. Not being sure about your career doesn’t mean you don’t have dreams or a future. You are you — and no one should dictate what your future should be.

We all have different paces and paths. I hope we don’t pressure ourselves too much. Let’s take one step at a time and focus on the present. I believe that we will all become successful in our own careers — careers that we will truly enjoy. This is not just a message for the readers, but also a reminder to myself.
