🌍 My Dream Places to Visit

🇯🇵 Japan

Japan is known for its rich culture, cherry blossoms, advanced technology, and traditional temples.

🇰🇷 South Korea

South Korea blends modern innovation with traditional heritage and is famous for K-pop and street food.

🇵🇭 Boracay, Philippines

Boracay is known for its white sand beaches and crystal-clear waters.

🇭🇰 Hong Kong

Hong Kong features an iconic skyline, vibrant culture, and amazing cuisine.

🇫🇷 Paris, France

Paris is known for romance, art, architecture, and the Eiffel Tower.
