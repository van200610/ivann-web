🏐 Spiking the Stress Out!

You know what’s harder than academics? Having no time for yourself. Sometimes we need to free ourselves from constant schoolwork because it can be very overwhelming. I personally make sure to relax at the end of the week. How? By playing volleyball.

Volleyball wasn’t my first sport. Basketball was my sport since I was three years old. But over time, I no longer felt the same spark. Everything changed during our Grade 11 sportsfest when I experienced playing volleyball — and we even became champions. From that moment on, I became hooked. Playing every weekend helped me stay physically active and mentally refreshed. When I entered college, it became my dream to join a university varsity team. Even though it didn’t happen when I entered UE, I continued improving myself by learning from better players. Volleyball is no longer just a hobby — it’s my passion. No matter how heavy college life gets, spiking the ball feels like spiking all the stress out.

What I’m saying is that we all need something that helps us release stress at least once a week. It doesn’t have to be a sport — just something that makes you happy and relaxed. Work-life balance matters. Life shouldn’t only revolve around school, work, or pressure. As we push through the entire week, we deserve something that makes us feel fulfilled.
